APRIL 26-29, 2019
Berlin, Germany

Embedded Systems and IoT in Rust.

One day of guided workshops for developers looking to start or improve their Embedded Rust skills, one day of talks by community members, and a two day development session focused on building Embedded Systems in Rust.

Schedule

Workshops

26 April

The OxidizeConf Workshops are a one day, two track set of workshops led by Ferrous Systems. Tickets for the OxidizeConf Workshops are sold separately from Conference tickets. The tracks will be divided as Beginner and Advanced Embedded Rustlang development. Participants will be provided all embedded hardware necessary for the course.
